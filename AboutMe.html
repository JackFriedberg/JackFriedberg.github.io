About Me

Jack Friedberg was born in Lafayette, Louisiana in 1998. He began his education in the French Immersion program, becoming fluent in the language by the age of 8, and continued his french education through high school. Jack tested into the Gifted and Talented program in elementary school and began taking core courses in an accelerated learning environment in middle school. He attended Lafayette High School, continuing with the Gifted and Talented program, where he realized his interest in math and science.Jack joined the Lafayette High Investment and Trading Club as a sophomore in high school, serving as Chairman of the Appropriations committee. His senior year of high school, Jack joined the Cajun Artificial Heart Lab, a biomedical engineering lab at his local university. This unique experience provided Jack with an opportunity to experiment with different types of engineering and gain valuable experience in research.

Currently, Jack is attending Texas A&M as a freshman engineering major. Following his passion for finance, Jack joined the Aggie Investment Club in the Fall of 2016. He, along with three other members of this club, won the university-wide Seeking Alpha Collegiate Stock Pitch Competition that same year.

In the future, Jack hopes to join the Master of Science in Finance program at Texas A&M and pursue a career in corporate finance or investment banking.
